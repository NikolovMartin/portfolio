Our Facilities

STRENGTH AREA

nThe power zone is 1000 square meters and is equipped with over 70 devices from the world-famous Life Fitness and Technogym. You can also benefit from a functional training area that is well-equipped with everything you need. The power and functional training areas are covered with the highest class of antibacterial flooring and air conditioned with a specially designed ventilation system to ensure maximum comfort for the visitors.

CARDIO AREA

The Cardio Zone combines over 50 brand new devices from the Life Fitness series, 2017 models. Here you will find instruments such as cross-trainers, steppers, paddle trainers, training bikes and more, all equipped with interactive multimedia with TV, radio and internet for the most enjoyable and varied workouts.

SPA CENTRE

The spa area is one of a kind and is the crown jewel of the Pulse Fitness Gym. On an area of 1000 sq.m we offer spa therapies, traditional and sports massages, peels, relaxing, slimming and exotic procedures. This is the place for absolute relaxation and you can choose to pamper yourself with: Himalayan sauna Finnish sauna Aroma steam bath Aroma sauna Panoramic steam bath Hammam
